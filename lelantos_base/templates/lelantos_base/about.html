Lelantos

One of the younger Titanes - god of moving unseen and a hunter's skill at stalking prey


This is a multi-purpose, extensible, platform for executing, orchestrating and analysing digital-footprint exploits









Motivation

As society becomes more and more reliant on online, vastly interconnected, networks it becomes impossible to navigate the modern world without leave behind a trail of digital data points - the digital footprint


This, increasingly information-rich, digital footprint can be used as a threat surface by bad actors for nefarious purposes including harvesting sensitive information, conducting offensive open-source intelligence (OSINT) operations, and gaining starting points for further exploits such as social engineering attacks


This projects aims to raise awareness of the risks presented through digital footprint exploitation using (and extending) readily available technologies to demonstrate the risk online users are exposed to, often without their knowledge.
